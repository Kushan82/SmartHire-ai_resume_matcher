Job Title: Data Analyst

We are looking for a Data Analyst to join our growing analytics team. The ideal candidate will have experience in working with large datasets, extracting insights, and visualizing them for business users.

Responsibilities:
- Query large datasets using SQL
- Build dashboards using Tableau or Power BI
- Perform A/B testing and statistical analysis
- Collaborate with business stakeholders to define KPIs

Skills Required:
- Proficient in SQL and Python
- Experience with BI tools (Tableau, Power BI)
- Strong analytical and communication skills

Preferred:
- Experience with retail or e-commerce data
- Understanding of machine learning basics
